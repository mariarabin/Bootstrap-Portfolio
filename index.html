About Me

Call me "Maria". Typical name, but not a typical person.

I seek to translate my boundless energy into dynamic-yet-functional contents that make browsing in websites a pleasurable experience. Just like how going to theme parks, driving fast (without breaking the speed limit), partying at the beach and shopping sprees can make people, especially me 😉...happy.

So I hope you enjoy exploring this new-born cyber crib. Feel free to drop some comments or suggestions. I will appreciate and use them as inspiration.
